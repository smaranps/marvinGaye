Marvin Gaye


Marvin Pentz Gay Jr. (Known as Marvin Gaye), is a black artist that is mostly known for composing motown music. He was the star of his generation and could awaken anyone just by singing. He was born on April 2nd,1931 Washington, D.C.in the United States of America. He was also given the nickname: Prince of Motown, and Prince of Soul. Here is his life in pictures.
